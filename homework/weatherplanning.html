Purpose

This site's purpose is to broadcast the weather from three towns, Franklin, Greenville, and Springfield, to the local community surrounding the area.

Audience Description

The general population that this site is aimed at are people in their 50s to 80s who live in one of the surrounding areas. They are active and are middle class. They are also retired.

Persona

Betty is a 72 year old woman who lives in Greenville and is retired. She loves to spend her time outdoors gardening and walking. Her husband has passed so she is looking for new past times to fill that void. She is a grandmother to children that live out of state so she keeps to herself. She is frequently checking the weather to know if she can go and spend time outside doing an activity of her choice.
